Advice for New Dog Parents :-
Tip #1 - Pay attention to your pup's excitement level. Helping them remain calm, or calm down after they get excited, can prevent a lot of headaches as they learn and grow.
Tip #2 - Don't overfeed your dog, it will significantly impact their health and happiness
Tip #3 - Have an emergency plan in place
Tip #4 - Walk your dog for both their health and happiness
Tip #5 - Consult your veterinarian before trying new foods or medications
